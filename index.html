Get your own Minecraft world in the cloud, publicly accessible via (username).m.ly. There's no setup at all and it's free forever. Just invite your family and friends to play with you anytime, anywhere!

Move fast

Minecraftly is a cloud platform that helps you quickly get an online Minecraft world for free, play with family, and grow your friendship. Minecraftly is made up of complementary features that you can mix-and-match to fit your needs.

Forget about infrastructure

Get a Minecraftly world is extremely quick and easy. You don't have to buy servers to host your online world, you don't have to care about CPU and RAM. With intuitive GUI packaged into a single server address, you can focus on building your own community and not waste time building complex infrastructure.

Connect everything

Interconnect is the free and unlimited world solution built directly into Minecraftly. Your inventories, player datas, etc... are connected cross worlds. You travel from your world to anyone elses' world seamlessly, and everything feels like you are in a giant interconnected community.

Getting started is easy!

Step 1 Step 2 Step 3

1. Open Minecraft PC client on Windows, Mac or Linux, then choose "Multiplayer" mode.

2. Click the "Add Server" button, simply put m.ly as server address. Click "Done".

3. That's it! Your world is automagically accessible by anyone via the address (username).m.ly
