Features

Minecraftly helps you get a world in the cloud, grow your user base, and store your world on our flash cloud-based storage in real time. Each feature works independently, and they work even better together.

Virtually Unlimited Storage

At the heart of Minecraftly is a global multi-petabyte scale storage. The Overworld, the Nether, and The End are generated for your username's UUID.
  • Each Overworld has a border of 10,240 blocks in radius. Which means the total size of your Overworld alone is over 40 million.
  • You can set a spawn point to your world via the simple command /setspawn. Others can go back to spawn via /spawn command.
  • Our storage cluster is built on flash technology to ensure your world loads blazing fast, all the time.
  • Reset your world at anytime via /reset command. Only your world is reset, other datas like inventories are kept safely.

Control With Ease

Build better community for yourself and leave the operations to us. Save crucial time keeping spammers and trolls out of your world.
  • Our Anti Ad Anti Spam is implemented at a proxy level, no action needed from your side.
  • Mute trolls via /mute command.
  • Kick trolls from your world via /kick command.
  • Ban a troll completely from your world via /ban command
  • We have Enterprise grade, machine learning DDoS protection in place, supported by Google Cloud Platform. We are in their Launchpad program and get full support so you don't have to worry about anything.

Cross world interactions

Unlike other solutions, where your inventories, XP and saved points are gone when you jump to other players' server/world, we keep all of your data consistent everywhere you go. It makes you feel that you are part of a giant community.

Grow

Acquire friends and followers. Engage and play with people you know and new friends at any time. Take the guesswork out of growing your community.
